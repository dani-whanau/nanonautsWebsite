Choosing an Instrument

If you need help choosing an instrument send us an email

Playing a concert can be great fun! Or can be totally scary! Sometimes both at the same time

So we've put together a list of top tips or great concerts!!!!!!!!!!

Copy out a set-list

A set-listis a list of all your songs and the order you will play them

Make copies for everyone!

Print it BIG!so that you can still read it if it's shown down by your feet on the floor or if the lighting isn't good.

Remember your spares

Some instruments have spare parts that need to be replaced if they wear out or break. For example:

  • guitar strings
  • saxophone or clarinet reeds
  • drumsticks

Make a list of the spares you may need and make sure you know where they are in an emergency

Plan where you'll be on stage

Before you start to set up your instruments on stage, take a few minutes to decide where you'll all be going to be.

  • Will the drums be center stage?
  • Is there a power socket somewhere?

It's much better to think about all this before you star setting up your instruments

No where you are going

If you are playing a concert somewhere you have never been before make sure you know where it is.

And when you do arrive the first thing you should do is find out what time you're on. Don't be caught out!
