Practice, practice, practice!

If you want to get good at something you need to practice - ALOT!


Here is a list of things you can do to get better:

  • Set aside time to play
  • These can be minutes
  • These can be hours
  • Make sure to take breaks when you get tired
  • Repeat every day

If you hit a wrong note or make a mistake, don't stop playing - just carry on and look as though you meant it!
